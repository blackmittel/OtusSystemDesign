=== Схема взаимодействия

==== Принципы взаимодействия

[cols="2,3,4",options="header"]
|===
| Тип | Когда используется | Протокол
| Синхронный | Мгновенный ответ, строгая консистентность | HTTPS (REST), gRPC
| Асинхронный | Устойчивость к сбоям, fan-out, eventual consistency | Kafka
| Webhook | Внешний провайдер инициирует событие | HTTPS
|===

==== Синхронные взаимодействия (прямые вызовы сервис->сервис)

[cols="1,2,2",options="header"]
|===
| Компонент A | Компонент B | Протокол
| Client | GeoDNS | DNS
| GeoDNS | API Gateway | HTTPS
| Client | CDN | HTTPS
| API Gateway | Auth Service | gRPC
| API Gateway | User Service | HTTP
| API Gateway | Ticket Service | HTTP
| API Gateway | Search Service | HTTP
| API Gateway | Order Service | HTTP
| API Gateway | Payment Service | HTTP
| API Gateway | Redis | RESP
| Order Service | Ticket Service | gRPC
| Order Service | Payment Service | gRPC
| Order Service | Delivery Service | gRPC
| Billing Service | Payment Service | gRPC
|===

==== Асинхронные взаимодействия (через Kafka)

===== Producers Kafka

[cols="1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| Producer | Topic | События
| Ticket Service | ticket.events | ticket.created, ticket.updated, ticket.published, ticket.deleted, ticket.reserved
| Ticket Service | message.events | message.created, message.sent, message.delivered, message.read
| Order Service | order.events | order.created, order.confirmed, order.cancelled, order.completed, order.shipped
| Payment Service | payment.events | payment.created, payment.processing, payment.succeeded, payment.failed, payment.refunded
| Delivery Service | delivery.events | delivery.created, delivery.in_transit, delivery.completed, delivery.failed
|===

===== Consumers Kafka

[cols="1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| Consumer | Topic | Действие
| Search Service | ticket.events | Индексация объявлений в ElasticSearch
| Order Service | payment.events | Обновление статуса заказа после оплаты
| Order Service | delivery.events | Обновление статуса заказа по доставке
| Billing Service | order.events | Расчёт комиссии и выплата продавцу после order.completed
| Analytics Service (ClickHouse) | ticket.events | Сбор аналитики по объявлениям
| Analytics Service (ClickHouse) | order.events | Сбор аналитики по заказам
| Analytics Service (ClickHouse) | payment.events | Сбор аналитики по платежам
| Analytics Service (ClickHouse) | delivery.events | Сбор аналитики по доставке
| Analytics Service (ClickHouse) | message.events | Сбор аналитики по сообщениям
|===

===== Полная схема Kafka (Producer -> Topic -> Consumer)

[cols="1,2,1",options="header"]
|===
| Producer | Topic | Consumer
| Ticket Service | ticket.events | Search Service
| Ticket Service | ticket.events | Analytics Service (ClickHouse)
| Ticket Service | message.events | Analytics Service (ClickHouse)
| Order Service | order.events | Billing Service
| Order Service | order.events | Analytics Service (ClickHouse)
| Payment Service | payment.events | Order Service
| Payment Service | payment.events | Analytics Service (ClickHouse)
| Delivery Service | delivery.events | Order Service
| Delivery Service | delivery.events | Analytics Service (ClickHouse)
|===

===== Topics и партиционирование

[cols="1,2,2",options="header"]
|===
| Topic | Ключ партиционирования | Retention
| ticket.events | ticket_id | 7 дней
| message.events | conversation_id | 7 дней
| order.events | order_id | 7 дней
| payment.events | payment_id | 30 дней
| delivery.events | delivery_id | 7 дней
|===

===== Consumer группы

[cols="1,2",options="header"]
|===
| Consumer группа | Топики
| search-service-group | ticket.events
| order-service-group | payment.events, delivery.events
| billing-service-group | order.events
| analytics-service-group | ticket.events, order.events, payment.events, delivery.events, message.events
|===

==== Внешние взаимодействия (Webhook)

[cols="1,2,2",options="header"]
|===
| Компонент A | Компонент B | Протокол
| Платёжный агрегатор | Payment Service | HTTPS (webhook)
| Логистический API | Delivery Service | HTTPS (webhook)
|===
